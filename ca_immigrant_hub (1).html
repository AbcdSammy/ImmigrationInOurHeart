🚗 Getting Your California Driver License

📌 Official Resources:
CA DMV Official Website | Driver Handbook
📅 Last Updated: Jan 2026
💰 Cost: $41
⏱️ Timeline: 2-4 weeks

WHO needs a CA driver license?

Anyone who becomes a California resident and wants to drive must get a CA license within 10 days of arriving.

WHAT documents do you need?

  • Valid Passport or Birth Certificate
  • Two proofs of CA residency (Utility bill, Lease)
  • Social Security Number (if eligible)

🆔 Social Security Number

How to Apply

You must visit a Social Security Administration (SSA) office in person with your original immigration documents.

  • Passport with Visa
  • I-94 Form
  • Work Authorization (if applicable)
